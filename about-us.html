Esterling Accime

PG-ecom Founder, United States

Esterling founded Protege Global E-commerce (PG-ecom), a skills training organization that creates content and courses around e-commerce. Courses are provided in Haitian Creole to help Haitians launch, create and manage successful online businesses. Esterling is also co-founder of Ecom Builder that helps customers start their e-commerce business with the right high-conversion creative and assets.
